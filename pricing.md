# Pricing — FrontDesk (frontdesk.works)

> FrontDesk is an AI front desk / AI receptionist for appointment-based businesses (med spas, salons, dental & aesthetic clinics, physio/wellness, and home-service trades like plumbers and HVAC). It answers missed calls (instant text-back), website chat, and Instagram DMs, qualifies and follows up with leads, and books appointments into the calendar — 24/7.

## Plan
- **Pricing:** Custom — tailored to each business
- **How it's priced:** based on your call/inquiry volume, the channels you need (calls, chat, Instagram, booking page), and your services
- **Setup fee:** $0 (no setup fee)
- **Contract:** None — cancel anytime
- **Guarantee:** If it doesn't pay for itself in month one, you don't pay
- **How to get a quote:** Book a 15-minute demo at https://frontdesk.works/#demo

## Included in every plan
- Website chat widget (one-line embed, works on any site)
- Missed-call → instant text-back (SMS follow-up that books)
- Instagram DM auto-reply
- Online / self-serve booking page
- 24/7 AI answering, lead qualification, and appointment booking
- Syncs with your existing calendar & booking system
- Trained on your services, prices, and policies
- Monthly recovered-revenue report
- Done-for-you setup (typically live within a week)

## Who it's for
Appointment-based and inbound-lead businesses where a missed message means a missed booking: med spas, aesthetic and dental clinics, hair/nail salons and barbershops, physiotherapy and wellness studios, and home-service trades (plumbers, HVAC, electricians).
