When every stylist is behind the chair, nobody's answering the phone — and the caller books somewhere else. FrontDesk catches those calls, texts, and DMs and fills your chairs, 24/7.

FrontDesk is an AI receptionist for salons, barbershops, and nail studios. It answers website chat, missed calls (via instant text-back), and Instagram DMs, quotes your service prices, and books appointments straight into your calendar — with custom flat-rate pricing, no setup fee.

Where salons lose bookings

  • Everyone's busy with clients. The phone rings out during peak hours — exactly when new clients are trying to book.
  • Walk-in-now demand. "Any openings today?" texts and DMs go unanswered until it's too late.
  • Evenings & Sundays. People book their week when you're closed.

What FrontDesk does

  • Texts back every missed call in seconds and books by SMS.
  • Answers "how much for a cut & color?" instantly from your price list.
  • Turns Instagram and website chat into booked appointments, around the clock.
  • Reduces no-shows with automatic confirmations and reminders.
