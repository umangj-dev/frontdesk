Choosing an AI receptionist that actually books appointments

Updated June 2026

An AI receptionist is software that answers inquiries and handles routine front-desk work automatically. The category is crowded — and most options only answer the phone. Here's what separates a tool that books revenue from one that just talks.

FrontDesk is an AI receptionist built for appointment businesses — med spas, salons, clinics, and home-service trades. It answers calls (via instant text-back), website chat, and Instagram DMs in about 60 seconds, quotes your prices, and books the appointment into your calendar, 24/7, for a flat $750/month.

What to look for

  1. It books, not just chats. Writing the appointment into your real calendar is the whole point. Many tools stop at "I'll have someone call you."
  2. It covers more than the phone. A lot of demand now arrives by text and Instagram DM. Phone-only AI misses it.
  3. It knows your prices. "Botox starts at $12/unit" closes; "someone will get back to you" doesn't.
  4. Flat, predictable pricing. Per-minute models punish you for being busy.
  5. Graceful human handoff for sensitive or unusual requests.
  6. Done-for-you setup — you shouldn't have to train an AI yourself.
